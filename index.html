The problem we solve

Family care is fragmented.
It doesn't have to be.

When an elderly parent needs help, the responsibility for coordination typically falls on one person in the family. It's time-consuming, stressful — and hard to share with everyone else.

KinCare brings everything together in one place, so you can spend your energy on what matters — being there for each other.

8–12 hours per week
spent on average by family carers coordinating care — phone calls, messages and manual bookings across multiple providers.
Over 60% don't live nearby
Many families coordinate care from a distance — with no real-time overview of what is actually happening day to day.
4–8 separate providers
A typical person with care needs uses transport, meals, cleaning, companionship and health services — from providers that don't talk to each other.
The family isn't coordinated
Siblings and other family members lack a shared overview — and one person ends up carrying everything alone.
How it works

From chaos to clarity — in three steps

KinCare is designed to be simple for everyone in the family — regardless of digital experience.

01
Create your family dashboard
Invite siblings and other family members with one click. Everyone shares one overview — wherever they live.
02
Find and book vetted providers
Choose from a network of screened local providers — transport, meals, cleaning, health services and much more.
03
Stay informed in real time
Receive notifications, view the activity log and share updates with the family — everything in one place.

Q&A

Frequently asked questions

We are in full development and expect a closed beta in autumn 2026 for early users. Everyone who signs up now will be prioritised and contacted when the beta opens.
We are still finalising our pricing model. Early users who sign up now will get free access for the first 6 months and are guaranteed the lowest price at launch. Signing up costs nothing.
At launch we support transport, meal delivery, cleaning and companionship. We are working to expand with personal care, physiotherapy, hairdressing and health services. All providers are screened before being admitted to the platform.
Council integration is on our roadmap and is expected to be ready after the initial launch. We are working on an open API solution that can integrate with the most widely used municipal care systems, so private and public care appear in one unified overview.
KinCare is built with GDPR as a foundation. Data is stored on European servers, we never sell data to third parties, and you can request a full export or deletion of your data at any time. We work with a certified data protection officer.
Your loved one doesn't need to use the platform at all for the family to benefit from it. If they do wish to use it, we have a dedicated tablet interface with very large text, a voice guide and only the most essential features — it can be shown to them and explored together.
